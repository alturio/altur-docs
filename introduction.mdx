## Main Features and Components

Altur is designed to empower your organization by automating mass communications. Our platform utilizes chatbots and AI agents to streamline interactions with clients across various areas, including sales, marketing, support, and collections. By providing specific instructions, information, and tools, our AI agents effectively manage and enhance your communication processes.
